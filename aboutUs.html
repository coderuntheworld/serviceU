About Us

Our Mission

Service U was birthed from the personal challenges of our founder, Donald Drumpf, to gain access to the businesses and services he needed outside traditional business hours and locations. Here at Service U our primary mission is to provide you with WHAT you need, WHEN and WHERE you need it!


At Service U we are all about the CCD code: Connection. Communication. Delivery.

We believe that providing a high standard of service leads to a high quality base of clients and customers, ensuring the longevity and success of our business and benefit to our community for years to come.

Connection

We're here to connect everyday people with the services they need when they need them - right in the compfort of their own homes.
